Buying with PhotoShop!

  1. Download the PhotoShop app, or browse to the PhotoShop! website.
  2. Take a picture of the item you want to buy and PhotoShop! it.
  3. Purchase and enjoy!

Selling with PhotoShop!

  1. Create a Shopify Store.
  2. Upload your items to Shopify and product images to TinEye.
  3. Profit.

APIs Used

  • TinEye - for reverse image search
  • Shopify - for the test store
